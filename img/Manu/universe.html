Welcome to the Universe The Universe is all of space and time and their contents, including planets,stars,galaxies, and all other forms of matter and energy. The Big Bang theory is the prevailing cosmological description of the deveolopment of the universe. According to this theory,. space and time emerged 13.787 billion years ago, and the universe has been expanding ever since the Big Bang. While the spatial size of the entire universe is unknown, it is possible to measure the size of the observable universe.
